Instructions for initial use of system

Prototype is in an iframe. Once on the protype page click the login dropdown located at the top right. We do not have backend for login, so there are links at the bottom for each of the manager, referee, and captain pages. Once in each of the pages, feel free to browse the schedule, league, and player pages. To get back to home page either click the logo or logout in the dropdown at the top right.
